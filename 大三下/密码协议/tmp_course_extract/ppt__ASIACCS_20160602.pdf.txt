===== PAGE 32 =====
Conclusion
o We, for the first time, provide a taxonomy of
smart-card-loss attacks.
o We show that some critical criteria are unworkable
due to a number of ambiguities and redundancies.
o We further propose viable fixes and refinements
to make an through measurement possible.
o We provide a comparative evaluation of 26 two-
factor schemes based on the refined metric,
highlighting the design challenges and difficulties.
